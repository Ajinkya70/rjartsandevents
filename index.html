About me

Empty rooms scare
most people. They
excite me.

I'm a Production Designer and Art Director based in Mumbai. For over a decade, I've been transforming bare locations into emotionally rich, camera-ready environments. From the authentic middle-class interiors of Jersey to the eerie villages of Netflix's Kaala Paani — every set I build serves the story first.

I've designed sets for Bollywood films, OTT series, brand campaigns, and music videos. Whether it's a gritty political drama or a pastel pink Pond's beauty shoot — I bring the same obsessive attention to detail.

What I do

I design spaces
that cameras love.

01

Film & OTT Set Design

Complete production design for feature films and streaming series — concept sketches, scale models, and final on-set execution.

02

Brand Campaign Design

Visually compelling sets for advertising shoots, product campaigns, and commercials that elevate brand identity and tell product stories.

03

Set Concept & Art Direction

Developing the visual language, mood boards, and concept art that define the world of a project before construction begins.

04

Location Transformation

Dramatic before-and-after space makeovers — taking real locations and reimagining them into completely new environments.

05

Music Video Production Design

Atmosphere-driven sets for music videos that enhance the emotional arc and visual storytelling of each track.

06

Prop Styling & Spatial Design

Meticulous prop sourcing, custom fabrication, and spatial arrangement that brings authenticity and depth to every frame.

How it works

From script
to screen-ready.

01

Script & Brief

Deep-dive into the script, character arcs, emotional tones, and director's vision.

02

Mood & Concept

Visual references, colour palettes, mood boards. This is where the world's aesthetic takes shape.

03

Scale Models

Physical models and detailed floor plans so the team can visualize the space before build.

04

Location Recce

Scouting real locations, planning what stays, what changes, what gets built from scratch.

05

Execution

Hands-on construction, prop placement, and real-time art direction until every frame is perfect.
